Model 2- Functionality:

1. In the first EditText, you must insert your username.

2. In the second EditText, you must insert your password.

3. The first Button "Login" represents the login command. 
If you want to access your account, after you entered the 
username and the password you must click to this button.

4. The second Button "Register" gives you the possibility  
to acces registration form, if you don't have an account.
